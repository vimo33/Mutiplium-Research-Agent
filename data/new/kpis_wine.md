## 1) Grape Production (Viticulture)

| KPI | Why it matters | Definition & Formula | Unit | Good/Target (contextual) | Evidence / Data Sources |
|---|---|---|---:|---|---|
| **Yield per hectare** | Drives revenue & COGS | Total harvested kilos ÷ planted hectares | t/ha | Varietal/region dependent (e.g., premium Pinot 4–8 t/ha) | Vineyard ERP, weighbridge; agronomy logs |
| **Grape maturity index** | Predicts wine style & quality | Composite of Brix, TA, pH at harvest | °Brix / g/L / pH | Window per style (e.g., sparkling 18–20°B, still 22–25°B) | Lab tests; sampling protocol |
| **Disease incidence rate** | Reduces loss & quality risk | Infected vines ÷ total vines | % | < 2–5% during critical phenological stages | Scout reports; IPM records |
| **Irrigation water intensity** | Resource & sustainability KPI | Irrigation water applied ÷ tonne of grapes | m³/t | Benchmark vs. regional evapotranspiration | Flow meters, telemetry |
| **Labor productivity (harvest)** | Cost & timing critical | Tonnes picked ÷ labor hours | t/hr | Improve YoY by ≥10% with mechanization/training | Timekeeping; contractor invoices |
| **Chemical use intensity** | Compliance & certification | kg a.i. PPPs ÷ ha (or per tonne) | kg a.i./ha | Downward trend; aligned with organic/regen goals | Spray diaries; purchase logs |
| **Canopy variability (NDVI/CWI)** | Proxy for uniformity & yield | Coefficient of variation across blocks | % | CV ↓ YoY after interventions | Drone/satellite analytics |
