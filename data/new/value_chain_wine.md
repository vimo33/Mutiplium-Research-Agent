# Wine Industry Value Chain (Structured)

## 1. Grape Production (Viticulture)
**Actors:** Vineyard owners, grape farmers, cooperatives.  
**Activities:** Land acquisition & preparation; planting; trellising; irrigation; canopy management; pruning; pest/disease/IPM; soil/leaf sampling; in‑season nutrition; harvest (manual/mechanized).  
**Inputs:** Land & terroir suitability; rootstock/clone; water; fertilizers & biostimulants; crop protection; labor; machinery; vineyard tech (drones, NDVI, in‑field sensors, weather stations).  
**Value Drivers:** Grape quality (Brix/TA/pH, phenolics); yield/ha; disease pressure; labor efficiency; sustainability certifications (organic, biodynamic, SWNZ, HVE).

---

## 2. Wine Production (Vinification)
**Actors:** Wineries (boutique → industrial), cooperatives, custom crush/contract winemakers.  
**Activities:** Sorting; crushing/pressing; maceration; alcoholic & malolactic fermentation; lees management; racking; fining; filtration; stabilization; barrel/steel/amphora aging; blending; QA/QC.  
**Inputs:** Grapes/must; yeast/bacteria; barrels & tanks; enzymes/fining agents; inert gas; cleaning chemicals; energy; enology expertise; lab equipment.  
**Value Drivers:** Sensory quality; consistency across vintages; throughput & yield; cost of goods; brand reputation; certifications (AOC/DOC/DOP, GI); tech adoption (in‑tank sensors, NIR, inline analytics).

---

## 3. Packaging & Bottling
**Actors:** In‑house lines, mobile bottlers, specialized plants, glass & closure suppliers.  
**Activities:** Bottle prep; filling; closure (cork/screw/synthetic); labeling & date coding; case packing; palletizing.  
**Inputs:** Glass/bottles; cans/alt containers; closures; labels; cartons; inks/adhesives.  
**Value Drivers:** Packaging design & premium cues; oxygen pickup control; line efficiency (OEE); defect rates; eco‑profile (lightweight/recycled glass, alt formats).

---

## 4. Distribution & Logistics
**Actors:** Importers, distributors, wholesalers, 3PLs, bonded warehouses, freight forwarders.  
**Activities:** Warehousing; inventory mgmt; domestic/international shipping; customs & excise; cold‑chain for premium wines; last‑mile delivery.  
**Inputs:** Transport networks; bonded storage; compliance; temperature/humidity control; ERP/WMS/TMS.  
**Value Drivers:** Global reach; on‑time/in‑full (OTIF); spoilage minimization; landed cost; access to fragmented retail channels.

---

## 5. Retail & Sales
**Channels:**  
- **Off‑trade:** Supermarkets, wine shops, convenience.  
- **On‑trade:** Restaurants, hotels, bars.  
- **Direct‑to‑consumer (DTC):** Tasting rooms, clubs, subscriptions, e‑commerce/marketplaces.  
**Value Drivers:** Brand strength; shelf placement; staff/sommelier advocacy; digital presence & conversion; repeat purchase/club retention; pricing & promo effectiveness.

---

## 6. Marketing & Branding
**Actors:** Wineries, distributors, agencies, PR, tourism operators.  
**Activities:** Brand strategy & storytelling; content; events; critic/influencer engagement; awards/ratings mgmt; wine tourism.  
**Value Drivers:** Distinctive story around terroir/craft; social & community engagement; earned media; awards/ratings impact; experiential marketing ROI.

---

## 7. Consumption
**Actors:** End consumers (individuals, collectors), on‑trade buyers.  
**Activities:** Purchase; consumption; storage/cellaring; community & review activity.  
**Value Drivers:** Consumer preference trends (premiumization vs. value); taste/style shifts (organic/natural/low‑alcohol); cultural associations; cellar performance.

---

## 8. Recycling & Aftermarket
**Actors:** Municipal & private recyclers, cork programs, secondary markets & auction houses.  
**Activities:** Bottle/glass recycling; cork take‑back; resale of rare/collectible wines; provenance verification.  
**Value Drivers:** Sustainability reputation; recovery rates; resale margins; authenticity & traceability.

---

## Cross‑Cutting Factors
**Regulation & Certification:** Appellations & GIs, label rules, alcohol taxes, sustainability schemes.  
**Technology:** Precision viticulture, IoT & analytics, AI recommendation engines, digital traceability (blockchain/QR/NFC).  
**Sustainability:** Organic/biodynamic/regen practices, water/energy/carbon intensity, circular packaging and logistics.
